  • Explain how the villi are adapted for absorption.
      • villi lining (epithelium) is only one cell thick → rate of diffusion increases
      • large surface area provided by having microvilli → rate of diffusion increases
      • protein channels for active transport
      • mitochondria for active transport
      • good blood supply with many capillaries
      • lacteal for fats absorption
  • Describe the function of mucus in villi.
      • prevents autodigestion, toxins, pathogens
      • prevents walls from being damaged from stomach acid
