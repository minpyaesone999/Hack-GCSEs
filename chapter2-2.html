Levels of organisation

  • State the function of ciliated cells.
    • movement of mucus in the trachea and bronchi
  • State the function of root hair cells.
    • absorption of water and ions
  • State the function of xylem vessels.
    • conduction of water and gives support to plant
  • State the function of palisade mesophyll cells.
    • photosynthesis
  • State the function of nerve cells.
    • conduction of impulses
  • State the function of red blood cells.
    • transport of oxygen
  • State the function of sperm and egg cells.
    • reproduction
  • Define tissue.
    • a group of cells with similar functions, working together to perform a shared function
  • Define organ.
    • a structure made up of a group of tissues, working together to perform specific functions
  • Explain why the leaf is an organ.
    • It is made up of a group of tissues, working together to perform specific functions
  • Define organ system.
    • a group of organs with related functions, working together to perform body functions
