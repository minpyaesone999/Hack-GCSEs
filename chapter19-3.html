Population Size

  • Define population.
      • group of organisms of one species
      • living in the same area
      • at the same time
  • Define community.
      • all of the populations of different species in an ecosystem
  • Define ecosystem.
      • a unit containing the community of organisms and their environment, interacting together
  • Explain why population of a certain species would increase exponentially.
      • reproduction rate is much greater than death rate
      • no limiting factors
      • little/no competition
      • plenty of nutrients and other resources
      • no/few predators
      • no/few diseases
      • no/little pollution
