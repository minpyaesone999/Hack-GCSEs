Biological molecules

  • List the chemical elements that make up carbohydrates, fats, and proteins.
    • carbohydrates
    • fats
    • proteins
  • Small molecules and their corresponding large molecules
    • glucose → starch/glycogen/cellulose
    • amino acids → proteins
    • fatty acids and glycerol → fats

    • See (Practical) Section
  • What is the primary role of water.
    • acts as a solvent in organisms for important life processes like excretion, digestion, and transport
  • Describe the structure of DNA.
      • two strands coiled together to form a double helix
      • each strand contain bases A, T, C, and G
      • A always pairs up with T; C always pairs up with G
      • cross-links between strands are formed by pairs of bases forming hydrogen bonds
