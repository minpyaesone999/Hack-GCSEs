Tropic Responses

  • Define gravitropism.
      • a response in which parts of a plant grows towards or away from gravity
  • Define phototropism.
      • a response in which parts of a plant grow towards of away from the direction from which light is coming
  • Explain the role of auxin in controlling shoot growth in response to light.
      • auxins are produced in shoot tip
      • it diffuses down the stem
      • auxins collect in the dark side
      • auxin stimulates cell elongation
      • greater cell elongation on side in the dark so shoot grows towards light
  • Explain the role of auxin in controlling stem growth in response to gravity.
      • auxin is made in the shoot tip
      • auxin diffuses away from the tip
      • it collects on the lower side of the stem due to gravity
      • auxin stimulates cell elongation
      • the stem grows upwards
