Nutrients

  • Explain why plants store starch.
      • as a store of energy when plant cannot photosynthesize, like in the winter
      • starch can be used for storing because it's insoluble
  • Describe the use of carbohydrates and amino acids in plants.
      • carbohydrates: cellulose for cell wall, starch for energy/resipiration, nectar to attact insects to flower
      • amino acids: to make proteins for growth, and enzymes
  • Describe and explain the symptoms of magnesium deficiency in plants.
      • yellow leaves → plants deficient in magnesium make less chlrophyll
      • stunted growth; less sugar available in plant → less photosynthesis - reduced glucose and amino acids (which are needed for growth)
  • Describe and explain the effects of nitrate ion deficiency on plant growth.
      • plants absorb nitrogen as nitrate ions
      • nitrogen is needed to make amino acids which combine to form proteins
      • proteins are needed for growth
      • no proteins, so plant doesn't grow
