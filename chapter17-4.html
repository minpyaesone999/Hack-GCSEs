Monohybrid Inheritance

  • Define genotype.
      • the genetic make-up of an organism in terms of the alleles present
  • Define phenotype.
      • the observable features of an organism
  • Define homozygous.
      • having two identical alleles of a particular gene
  • Define heterozygous.
      • having two different alleles of a particular gene
  • Define pure-breeding.
      • two identical homozygous individuals that breed together will be pure-breeding
  • Define dominant allele.
      • an allele that is expressed if it is present
  • Define recessive.
      • an allele that is only expressed when there is no dominant allele of the gene present
  • Define and describe co-dominance.
      • both alleles are expressed in the phenotype
      • the phenotype is different and intermediate
      • there are multiple alleles for one trait
  • Define sex-linked characteristic.
      • a characteristic in which the gene responsible is located on a sex chromosome
      • this makes it more common in more sex than in the other
      • e.g. color lindness
  • Explain what test cross is and how to use a test cross to identify an unknown genotype.
      • an organism showing dominant characteristic could be homozygous dominant, or heterozygous
      • cross it with one known to have homozygous recessive genotype for the same gene
      • if offspring shows recessive characteristic, dominant parent is heterozygous
