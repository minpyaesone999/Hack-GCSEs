Asexual Reproduction

  • Define asexual reproduction.
      • a process resulting in the production of genetically identical offspring
      • from one parent
  • Advantages of asexual reproduction
      • less genetic variation
      • offspring is better adapated to the environment
      • faster process than sexual reproduction
      • doesn't require partner
      • requires less energy
      • same quality for every product (for crop production
  • Disadvantages of asexual reproduction
      • less genetic variation
      • more likely to be wiped out by disease
      • might not thrive in new environment
      • more competition for local resources
      • negative traits are always passed on
